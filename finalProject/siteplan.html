Mytho Magic - Site Plan

Site Name

Mytho Magic - I chose this name because the site lets users explore mythology and discover which pantheon they belong to through a quiz. It feels adventurous, mystical, and matches the theme of legendary stories.

Optional Domain Availability: mythomagic.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to introduce users to major world mythologies — Greek, Roman, Norse, and Egyptian — and let them take a fun interactive quiz to discover which pantheon they align with. The site will include mythology summaries, deity spotlights, and a results page highlighting their “mythological match.”

Scenarios

  • “Which mythological pantheon do I belong to?”
  • “Where can I read about the different gods and mythological worlds?”

Color Scheme

These colors reflect the mysterious and ancient feel of mythology:

  • Primary: Midnight Blue (#1A2A40) - headers, navigation
  • Secondary: Gold (#D4A017) - accents, buttons
  • Neutral: Ivory (#F8F5E4) - background
  • Dark: Slate Black (#111111) - footer text

Typography

  • Headings: Spectral SC - Historical feel
  • Body Text: Crimson Pro - Book-style serif
